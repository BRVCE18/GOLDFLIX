Weathering With You

Hodaka runs away from his remote island home to Tokyo. The weather is unusually gloomy and rainy every day. He lives his days in isolation, but finally finds work as a writer for a mysterious occult magazine. Then one day, Hodaka meets Hina. This bright and strong-willed girl possesses a strange and wonderful ability: the power to change and manipulate the weather.
