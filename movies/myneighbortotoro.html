My Neighbor Totoro

In 1958 Japan, university professor Tatsuo Kusakabe and his two daughters, Satsuki and Mei, move into an old house to be closer to the hospital where the girls' mother, Yasuko, is recovering from a long-term illness. It follows the story of two young daughters of the professor and their interactions with friendly wood spirits in post-war rural Japan.

Animation / Fantasy | 1988
